Welcome to Knoxville's First Makerspace

Come invent, create, and have fun in our unique makerspace!

Be Creative. Be Inspired.

Families can come and enjoy different events at this makerspace.

Kids of all ages can play at any of our unique stations. Whether you are interested in Math or art, all of our facilities can meet your needs!

What You Can Expect

Our top of the line science labs, art studios, and technology centers create opportunities for kids to be interactive and involved in our activities. From amazing art supplies to Legos and electronics, kids will never get bored at our stations!

"We created this makerspace so that kids around the community can come here to be themselves and learn new things. Through our many activities, we are having an impact on people who come for our services."
- The Executive Director
