Plan Your Visit

Be a Part of the Fun

We invite your family to join us in Knoxville's finest makerspace. Come experience the variety of fun educational activities for all ages!

Location

Our address is 1234 Knoxville Lane, Knoxville, Tennessee, 12345.

Parking is on all sides of the building.

Wheelchair parking is available near the main entrances. See map of the makerspace for more details.

Admission


Fees are paid onsite
  • Children 3 and Younger-Admission is free.
  • Children Ages 4 to 12 - $12
  • Adults - $18
  • Seniors (Age 55 +) - $15
  • Military Discount Available
  • See membership options.

Hours of Operation

We are open on the following days:
  • Tuesdays - Fridays: 9:30 A.M. to 8:00 P.M.
  • Saturdays - Sundays: 9:00 A.M. to 8:00 P.M.
  • Closed on Mondays.
